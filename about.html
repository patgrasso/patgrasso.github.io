About

I am currently a Junior pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology.


I have a particular interest for full-stack web development, including everything from database interfacing to front-end design.


Much of my personal experience has been with JavaScript/Node.js and various frameworks, however my professional experience at iCIMS has consisted mostly of Java .


For the past year (two semesters) I have been the teaching assistant for CS 385 - Algorithms at Stevens.


I have competed twice now (2014 and 2015) in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest, representing Stevens.


From these experiences and many more, I have developed an understanding and appreciation for the beauty of various types of algorithms and data structures as well as a knack for identifying and comparing solutions to problems. I have a passion for learning concepts, frameworks, and various technologies that allow me to create software, whether it be a system program that interacts with an external device, or a web application with an aesthetically appealing user interface.
